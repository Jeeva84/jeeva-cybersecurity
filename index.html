About Me

After moving to Canada, I took on multiple jobs to support my family, never losing sight of my long-standing passion for cybersecurity. Despite the challenges of balancing work, studies, and family responsibilities, I remained committed to my dream — pursuing formal education and completing certifications in cybersecurity and network security.

My journey has been built on perseverance, continuous self-learning, and a genuine passion for protecting digital environments. Through hands-on labs, real-world projects, and independent study, I have developed the skills necessary to detect, respond to, and mitigate cybersecurity threats.

Today, I am eager to turn my hard-earned knowledge and dedication into meaningful contributions in the cybersecurity industry. I believe that every challenge is an opportunity to grow, and every learning experience brings me closer to becoming the cybersecurity professional I aspire to be.
