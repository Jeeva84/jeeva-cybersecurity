About Me

I am a highly motivated cybersecurity professional with strong hands-on experience in threat detection, security operations, and incident response. With a foundation built on formal education, industry-recognized certifications, and real-world projects, I bring a practical, resilient, and results-driven approach to cybersecurity challenges.

My journey has been shaped by perseverance and passion. After moving to Canada, I took on multiple jobs to support my family while simultaneously pursuing my long-standing dream of working in cybersecurity. Balancing work, studies, and family responsibilities taught me resilience, time management, and unwavering dedication — qualities that now define my professional character.

Through formal education in cybersecurity and network security, combined with industry certifications such as CompTIA CySA+ and ISC2 CC, I have developed practical expertise in the following areas:

  • Threat Detection & Analysis: Real-time monitoring, anomaly detection, and threat correlation
  • Security Tools: QRadar (SIEM), SentinelOne (XDR)
  • Log & Event Management: Centralized logging, parsing, and retention strategies
  • Vulnerability Management: Scanning, prioritization, and remediation of security risks
  • Incident Response: Alert triaging, root cause analysis, and documentation aligned with NIST 800-61
  • Network Security Architecture: VPNs, VLANs, segmentation, and secure protocol enforcement

In addition to academic learning, I have continuously honed my skills through hands-on labs, independent projects, and real-world threat simulations. This blend of practical experience and theoretical knowledge enables me to detect, respond to, and mitigate cybersecurity threats effectively and efficiently.

I believe that cybersecurity is not just about technology — it’s about proactive thinking, collaboration, and an endless commitment to learning. Every challenge has strengthened my ability to adapt, solve problems creatively, and protect organizations against emerging threats.

Today, I am eager to turn my hard-earned knowledge, work ethic, and passion for security into meaningful contributions for an organization that values innovation, resilience, and a proactive security posture. I am ready to take on new challenges, continue growing, and make a positive impact in the cybersecurity industry.
